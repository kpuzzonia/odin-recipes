Parmesan Basil Orzo

Description

This light and flavorful orzo dish features tender pasta tossed with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil. Itâ€™s a simple yet elegant side that pairs beautifully with a variety of mains, offering a creamy texture and a burst of herbaceous freshness in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 2 T butter
  • 1 1/2 cups orzo
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 t garlic powder
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 T chopped fresh basil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. In a large skillet, combine butter and orzo.
  2. Sauté over medium heat until orzo is golden brown, stirring frequently.
  3. Slowly stir in broth and bring to a boil.
  4. Cover and reduce heat to low.
  5. Simmer 20 minutes or until orzo is tender and almost all the broth has been absorbed.
  6. Remove from the heat.
  7. Stir in basil and parmesand cheese and salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Enjoy!
